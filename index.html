Let's Help Flood Affected People

Rain and panic are now synonymous with the people of Sylhet who have been suffering for an indefinite period of time. With the exception of relatively high elevations, most of the area is flooded. River erosion is taking place in Sunamganj, Companiganj and other haor areas. There is no network coverage, even the tin rice is completely submerged. Kanaighat, Zakiganj, Gowainghat, Chhatak under water. The situation in some places is so deplorable that the army has been called in to rescue them.

Know more about Ourselves

AIM Initiative Foundation is a registered, non-profit and volunteering organization established in 2016 that works to provide free academic education, skill development activities (Arts & Crafts, Sewing, Music, Computer Education and Physical Training) and healthcare facilities to 150 underprivileged children of Meradiya, Dhaka, Bangladesh. The organization works in education, health, environment and youth empowerment and addresses Sustainable Development Goals (SDG).
